The Artist

Priyanka Singh

About the Artist

Priyanka Singh is a painter and the founder of Krira Gallery — a space built on the belief that extraordinary art should reach anyone, anywhere, without walls.

Working across painting, watercolour, and mixed media, her practice is rooted in an investigation of material and time. Each work begins as a question about what a surface can hold — and how long it can hold it. Based between the studio and the landscape, her work has been collected internationally.
