About the artist

Priyanka
Singh

An artist and the founder of Krira Gallery — a space built on the belief that extraordinary art should reach anyone, anywhere, without walls.

"I paint to make sense of the world — its silences as much as its noise."

Working in oil and mixed media, her practice explores the tension between structure and emotion — geometric forms that dissolve at the edges, landscapes that carry memory rather than describe place.
