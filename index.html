About the artist

Priyanka
Singh

Priyanka Singh is a painter and the founder of Krira Gallery — a space built on the belief that extraordinary art should reach anyone, anywhere, without walls.

"I paint to make sense of the world — its silences as much as its noise."

Working primarily in oil and mixed media, Priyanka's practice explores the tension between structure and emotion — geometric forms that dissolve at the edges, landscapes that carry memory rather than describe place. Her work draws from the terrain of South India, from colour as a felt experience, and from the quiet discipline of making something by hand.

Before devoting herself fully to her practice, she spent years as an art educator and collector — developing an eye for work that resists easy categorisation. Krira Gallery was founded to give her own work, and the artists she admires, a home outside the traditional system.
